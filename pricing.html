  Why should I signup only by my Gmail account in free plan?
EasyLeadz is a crowdsourced contacts directory which means we gather data from our community of users. Everytime a user sign ups we aggregate their contacts from their Gmail contact book, anonymize the contacts, enrich them by our intelligent algorithms & add them to our central database for all community to use it for free. Its like give and take.
  What is the gurantee of quality of data?
Our motto is to deliver quality data to our community. Our algorithm mines social profiles for all contacts & companies. It then performs a MX Record, SMTP & Catch All check and removes contacts that do not pass the test. Only then it adds data to central system.

We rely on algorithms and our community to give the best possible data to each one of our community.
